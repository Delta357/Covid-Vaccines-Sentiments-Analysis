Objectives of Project


  • 01Public Sentiment Analysis

    The main objective of the project is to analyse public sentiments using Natural Language Processing. The data is scrapped from Twitter related to above vaccines and is processed and analyzed to obtain the sentiments of people for every vaccine listed above.Through this we will get to know which vaccine is most famous among public and the percentage of positive and negative sentiments among people.

  • 02 News Article Classification

    This objective states that we will be classifying the news articles on vaccines into different categories. Provided the news article , we will tell you whether this specific news article is related to Covaxin, Covishied, Moderna, Pfizer or Sputnik V.

  • 03 News Article Summarisation

    Summarising news articles to few lines to save the time of the reader.Given the article, we will tell you important facts about the vaccine. The intention is to create a coherent and fluent summary having only the main points outlined in the given article.
